Research, analysis, and website creation files for the **India AI Impact Summit 2026** — the largest global AI summit ever held in the Global South.

## About the Summit

The India AI Impact Summit 2026 is a five-day mega-event (Feb 16–20) at Bharat Mandapam, New Delhi, organized by MeitY and NASSCOM under the IndiaAI Mission. It features 20+ heads of state, 250,000+ expected visitors, 3,250+ speakers across 700+ sessions, and 600+ startups across 70,000 sq. metres of exhibition space.
